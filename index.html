The two bedroom suite is spread across 170 sq mt and truly defines the concept of “Space is Luxury”. Perched at a hill top, surrounded by 180 acres of living breathing rainforest. This modern suite caters to all your needs. Surrounded by large bay view windows on two sides, both the rooms give you a beautiful view of the valley right from your bed. The interiors of the room blend in perfectly with the natural ecosystem around! Equipped with a work station, a separate fire space with day bed seating area and all modern day amenities, the suite exuberates splendour. It is makes for a perfect weekend sojourn !
